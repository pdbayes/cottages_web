Self catering Holiday Cottages in The Beautiful Countryside of Cornwall

Tregrill Farm is situated in a small hamlet just 6 miles from the Cornish coast. From our tranquil setting you can visit the whole of Cornwall, South Devon, Bodmin Moor and Dartmoor, with countryside walks accessible directly from your door.

We are a family run business with 4 generations of our family living on site. Our aim is simple, to provide "better than home" accommodation to make your holiday as enjoyable and relaxing as possible. To help you settle in, we provide our guests with a small welcome basket of local goods and some basic provisions.

Our cottages have been converted from typical Cornish Slate barns that date back to 1824 and retain many original features. Our cottages are set in an enclosed courtyard, each with its own barbecue area and garden and all are well equipped with a dishwasher; washing machine; tumble dryer; flat screen TV and DVD player. We also provide fresh towels and linen for your convenience.

The stunning South Cornwall Coast, has many attractive fishing villages such as Polperro and Looe as well as the famous South West coastal path which are just a short drive away. The fishing port of Looe can also be reached by taking the scenic train journey from Liskeard (2 miles). The maritime city of Plymouth is only a 30 minute drive away with the National Marine Aquarium and its historic Barbican district.

Adrenalin Quarry and Karting World is just over a mile away. For indoor activities, feel free to make use of the games room complete with an antique full size snooker table, table tennis, darts board and hi-fi. The village of Menheniot with the tardis like Spar shop and pub is just Â½ a mile away.
